The Podium Master

The Podium Master is a one-stop shop company for training & facilitation. We are a private company that does soft skills training workshop as well as public speaking, where if a speaker is required to speak at a certain event, like a conference or a seminar. The name came about because when I first became a Public Speaker, I was trained by a Peaker's Club called Toast Master International. Inspired by this name, & to realized that this is actually my greatest strength, that is where i'm standing behind a Podium, The Podium Master was born. Our Target Market It is mostly the youth, but human being in general. Because all human beings needs the skills I offer. The main purpose for Podium Master is to develop, & to make it possible for others to have skiils that are necessary. Our end goal is to become a College for soft skills.
